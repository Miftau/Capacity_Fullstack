RECRUITING

In the process of making better Islamic society and community around the globe, DAARU-L-ILM consider the sayings that “An idle hand is a workshop of Satan“. Our society will be great and better if those with talents are able to find organizations to use their talents the way they like with proper guidance from experts in that field. DAARU-L-ILM thereby included recruitment in her programs to employ talented and hardworking Muslims into the educational field and other organizational posts and also uses the opportunity to train the less talented but hardworking Muslims that are ready to learn to make them realize their dreams by the grace of Allah.
This recruiting process has procedures with no bias. Individuals are employed based on merit and capability and not on familiarity or favoritism as DAARU-L-ILM‘s administrative panel believed that is the only way to make the world better by setting the opportunity for people that have nobody to become somebody without needing to necessarily know anybody.
Whenever there are job vacancies at DAARU-L-ISLAMIC ISLAMIC STUDIES, they will be placed on our events and blog pages and applicants will be able to complete their application processes online.
